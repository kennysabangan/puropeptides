Support FAQ

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all email inquiries within 24 hours on business days (Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm EST). For urgent order issues, please include your order number in the subject line for priority handling.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also check your order status by emailing support@aminoclub.com with your order number. Orders are processed within 0-2 business days, with most arriving within 3-5 business days from fulfillment. Every order includes free shipment protection.

I received the wrong item or my order is damaged. What should I do?

We're sorry to hear that! Please email support@aminoclub.com immediately with your order number and photos of the damage. Photo evidence is required for all claims. Once verified, we'll send a one-time replacement within 24 hours at no cost. One replacement per customer per order, subject to review.

How can I verify the quality of my order?

Every order includes a batch-specific Certificate of Analysis (CoA) verifying purity, identity, and quality. You can also access CoAs on each product page. If you have questions about a specific CoA or need additional documentation, our support team is happy to help at support@aminoclub.com.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer competitive pricing for bulk research orders. Please email support@aminoclub.com with details about the products and quantities you're interested in. We'll provide a custom quote within 24-48 hours. Bulk orders may also qualify for expedited shipping and priority support.

Can you recommend products for my research?

While we cannot provide medical or therapeutic advice, our team can help you understand the specifications, purity levels, and research applications of our peptides. Email us with details about your research focus, and we'll point you toward relevant product information and published studies.

How do I get a Certificate of Analysis (CoA) for my order?

Every order includes access to batch-specific Certificates of Analysis. CoAs are available on each product page and are also included in your order confirmation email. If you need a specific CoA or have questions about lab results, email us with your order number and we'll send it right over.

My payment was declined. What should I do?

Payment declines can occur for several reasons. First, ensure your billing address matches your card exactly. If issues persist, try a different payment method or contact your bank to authorize the transaction. You can also email us and we'll help troubleshoot or provide alternative payment options.

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is ready to help with any questions about your research needs.
