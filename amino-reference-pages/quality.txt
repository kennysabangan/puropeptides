Our Quality Process

A rigorous six-step process ensures every peptide meets our exacting standards.

01
Raw Material Verification

All raw materials undergo identity and purity testing before synthesis begins. We verify amino acid purity, solvent quality, and reagent specifications.

02
Peptide Synthesis

Solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS) in controlled cleanroom environments. Each synthesis follows validated protocols with real-time monitoring.

03
Purification

Reverse-phase HPLC purification removes impurities and ensures target purity levels. Multiple purification passes when necessary.

04
Identity & Content Testing

HPLC content analysis and Mass Spectrometry identity confirmation on every batch.

05
Documentation

Full Certificate of Analysis generated with all test results. Batch records maintained for complete traceability.

06
Packaging & Storage

Lyophilized peptides sealed in sterile vials under nitrogen. Climate-controlled storage until shipment.
